Creating Web Pages Dynamically

Dynamic web site design can range in various levels of complexity. Simple dynamic pages such as this only use the minimal components. More advanceddynamic web site can include all parts of the page constructed including the content itself. Often timesdynamic web site also involve the use of a database to store the pages content. Depending on who you are or what you are looking for, the appropriate page structure and content can be loaded out of the database.

Dynamic design allows you to create more interactive web pages that respond to the actions of the user. Dynamicpages can sometimes take on the characteristics of applications rather than staticweb pages. Once you know Style Sheets and you know JavaScript, you can then start learning how to combine the features of both and apply advanced styles based on specific events.
