Researching on the Web

The Internet contains more information than any library in the world and is a great source for research. You can find information on anything on the Internet. Because of the massive amounts of information available, it is easy to get overwhelmed by information and never find what you’re actually looking for simply because you don't know where to look or even how search for what you're looking for.

When using the web for research, there area number of different options available to you. You can search for web sites with information on your topic using search engines, you can search for information using directories to fine other research and articles on your topic. Search Engines allow you to search for web sites and information based on key words and phrases. Directories let you navigate to what you're looking for by starting with a general topic and selecting more specific topics.
